The Wizard of Oz
   by L. Frank Baum

There once was a girl named Dorothy.
She lived in the prarie in Kansas with her uncle and aunt.
   (prarie 草原 cǎo yuán)
   (uncle 叔叔 shū shu)
   (aunt 阿姨 Ā yí)
One day, a strong tornado brought Dorothy, her dog Toto and her wooden cabin together to the sky.
   (strong 强的 qiáng de)
   (tornado 龙卷风 lóng juǎn fēng)
   (brought 带来了 dài lái le)
   (wooden 木制的 mù zhì de)
   (cabin 小屋 xiǎo wū)
   (together 一起 yī qǐ)
   (sky 天空 tiān kōng)

The tornado brought Dorothy to Munchkinland.
Her cabin crushed the Wicked Witch of the East when it landed.
   (crushed 压碎 yā suì)
   (Wicked 邪恶 xié è)
   (Witch 巫婆 wū pó)
   (landed 降落 jiàng luò)
So she had freed the local people.
   (local people 当地人 dāng dì rén)
   (freed 解放 jiě fàng)
So the Good Witch of the North gave her a pair of magic silver shoes.
   (magic 魔法 mó fǎ)
   (shoes 鞋子 xié zi)

Dorothy asked, "Can you take me home?"
   (take 拿 ná)
The good witch said: "Walk along this golden brick road to the Emerald City, and Oz will help you."
   (witch 巫婆 wū pó)
   (brick 砖 zhuān)
   (road 马路 mǎ lù)
   (Emerald 翠 cuì)
   (help 帮助 bāng zhù)
Dorothy took Toto on the road.
   (took 拿了 ná le)

On the way, Dorothy met a scarecrow who wanted a brain, a tin man who wanted a heart, and a cowardly lion who wanted courage.
   (scarecrow 稻草人 dào cǎo rén)
   (brain 脑子 nǎo zi)
   (tin 锡 xī)
   (heart 心脏 xīn zàng)
   (cowardly 懦夫 nuò fū)
   (courage 勇气 yǒng qì)

They came to the Emerald City together, and were fascinated by this beautiful city as soon as they entered the city.
   (as soon as 立刻 lì kè)
   (fascinated 吸引 xī yǐn)
   (entered 进入 jìn rù)
Here, the houses were all built with green emeralds.
   (all 所有 suǒ yǒu)
   (built 建造 jiàn zào)
   (emeralds 翠 cuì)

They came to the palace and waited for a meeting with Oz.
   (palace 宫殿 gōng diàn)
   (meeting 会议 huì yì)

Oz was willing to help everyone, but he required them to first get rid of the Wicked Witch of the West.
   (get rid of 摆脱 bǎi tuō)
   (willing 愿意的 yuàn yì de)
   (required 需求 xū qiú)
Dorothy agreed .
   (agreed 同意 tóng yì)

The Wicked Witch of the West knew Dorothy and the others wanted to kill her, so she sent flying monkeys to catch them.
   (catch 抓住 zhuā zhù)
   (kill 杀 shā)
   (sent 发送 fā sòng)

The flying monkeys caught them, and Dorothy became a slave to the Wicked Witch.
   (became 变成了 biàn chéng le)
   (slave 奴隶 nú lì)

One day, the wicked witch stole one of Dorothy's silver shoes.
   (wicked 邪恶 xié è)
   (stole 偷了 tōu le)
Dorothy was very angry and threw a bucket of water at the wicked witch.
   (angry 生气的 shēng qì de)
   (bucket 水桶 shuǐ tǒng)
The wicked witch melted unexpectedly.
   (melted 熔化 róng huà)
   (unexpectedly 意外 yì wài)

Dorothy and her friends returned to the Emerald City.
   (returned 返回 fǎn huí)
Oz gave the Scarecrow a brain made of needles and nails.
   (Scarecrow 稻草人 dào cǎo rén)
   (needles 针 zhēn)
   (nails 钉子 dīng zi)
He put a silk red heart into the Tin Man's chest.
   (silk 丝绸 sī chóu)
   (into 进入 jìn rù)
   (Tin 锡 xī)
   (chest 胸部 xiōng bù)
He took out a bottle of courage potion and let the Cowardly Lion drink it.
   (bottle 瓶子 píng zi)
   (potion 药水 yào shuǐ)
   (let 让 ràng)
   (Cowardly 懦夫 nuò fū)
   (drink 喝 hē)

But Oz couldn't send Dorothy home, she was very sad.
   (send 发送 fā sòng)
   (sad 伤心 shāng xīn)
A green man said to Dorothy, "The Good Witch of the South may be able to help you."
   (may be able 也许可以 yě xǔ kě yǐ)

The Good Witch of the South said to Dorothy: "Actually, you can go home anytime you want, as long as you bump the heels of your silver shoes together three times."
   (anytime you want 随时都可以 suí shí dōu kě yǐ)
   (Actually 其实 qí shí)
   (bump 撞 zhuàng)
   (heels 脚跟 jiǎo gēn)

Dorothy did it, and immediately flew up, like a tornado, back to Kansas.
   (immediately 立即地 lì jí de)
